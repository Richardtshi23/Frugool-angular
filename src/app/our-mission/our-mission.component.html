The "Loyalty Tax"

Our founder was obsessed with technology. He was the first in line for the latest gadgets, the early adopter, the loyal supporter. He spent years championing brands, telling his friends about the best new tech, and building his smart home piece by piece. Yet, after all that time and money, he realized something profound: his loyalty meant nothing. He was just a transaction, a data point on a spreadsheet. The brands he loved never once said "thank you" in a meaningful way.

The Spark of an Idea

That frustration became a question: "What if a company actually valued its supporters? What if we built a brand that wasn't just about the next sale, but about the long-term relationship?" That question was the spark that ignited Frugool. A mission to not only find the best, most innovative tech to solve real South African problems, but to build a community that genuinely rewards the people who believe in it.

Our Core Belief

We believe loyalty should be a two-way street.

We believe that when you support us, we should support you right back.

We believe a great product is only half the story; a great community is the other.

This Is Our Promise To You

At Frugool, we're building the brand we always wished existed. This means our mission is twofold: to bring you incredible, life-improving technology, and to show our genuine appreciation for your support.

We Reward Loyalty. Period.

This isn't just a marketing slogan. We actively surprise our loyal customers with free gifts, exclusive early access to new tech, and members-only giveaways. When you join the Frugool family, you're not just a customer—you're a valued partner.

We Also Give Back, Together.

A great community also cares for others. That's why we've pledged to donate 40% of all profits to orphanages and animal charities. Your purchase doesn't just upgrade your life; it provides hope for those who need it most.
