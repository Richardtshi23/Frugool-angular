Shipping & Returns

Your guide to receiving and returning your Frugool gadgets.

Shipping Policy

We are excited for you to upgrade your life with our smart gadgets! All orders are processed and dispatched from our local warehouse with care.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after you receive your order confirmation email.

Shipping Times & Rates

We offer a simple, flat-rate shipping option nationwide. All orders are shipped via a tracked courier service.

  • Standard Insured Shipping (1-5 Business Days): R63.99
  • Orders over R1000: FREE

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email notification from us which will include a tracking number you can use to check its status.

Returns & Refunds Policy

We stand behind the quality of our products. We accept returns up to **21 days** after delivery, if the item is unused and in its original condition, but only in the case of a damaged or incorrect product.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

In the unlikely event that your order arrives damaged in any way, or if you received the wrong item, please email us as soon as possible at **supportfrugool.com** with your order number and a photo of the item’s condition. We address these on a case-by-case basis but will do our best to work towards a satisfactory solution, be it a replacement or a full refund.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return for a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our support team. If your return is accepted, we’ll send you a return shipping label, as well as instructions on how and where to send your package.

Please note: We do not accept returns for change-of-mind or if the product has been used. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

Quick Summary

Shipping Time? 1-5 Business Days, Nationwide.
Return Window? 21 days for damaged or wrong items.
Change of Mind? Unfortunately, we do not accept returns for change-of-mind.
